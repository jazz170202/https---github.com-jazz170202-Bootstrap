Bay of bones

As menacing as the Bay of Bones sounds, the replicated floating village is far from it. While once an ancient settlement, its current state is a perfect combination of beauty and history. It’s located on the incredible Lake Ohrid and shows you a glimpse of what life was like so many years ago. You’ll have the chance to see model prehistoric homes, primitive tools, and (of course) broken bones.
